# Waystar Negotiation Strategy

## Discount Guardrails
- Standard: 10% off list
- Maximum: 20% off list (requires approval)
- Multi-year: Additional 5% per year

## Key Points
- Current renewal at risk due to budget constraints
- Competitor offering aggressive pricing
- Need to preserve relationship while protecting margin

## Approach
1. Bundle additional services to justify discount
2. Offer extended payment terms
3. Highlight ROI and past value delivered
